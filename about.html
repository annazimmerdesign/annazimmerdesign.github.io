About Me

Anna is currently an MFA student of Design and Technology at Parsons, working at the intersection of creative coding and critical design.

 

Through creative coding, physical computing, biomaterials, and speculative storytelling, Anna creates work that questions anthropocentric technological solutions while embracing radical optimism in her practice. She's particularly interested in how analog media can serve as meaningful counterpoints to digital infrastructure in our current volatile information environment.

 

Anna Zimmer is based in the New York-New Jersey area and can be contacted via any of the channels listed below.
